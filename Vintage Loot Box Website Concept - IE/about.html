About Vintage Loot Box


Vintage loot box is a premiere subscription service for the pop culture vintage collector. When you sign up for our subscription service, each month you will receive a box valued at $100 containing vintage collectibles consisting of toys, comics, VHS tapes, video games, trading cards, books, and other pop culture collectibles. Half of your items in the box will come from either the 1970's, 1980's, or 1990's. While the other half of the box will contain retro items from the 2000's to current. Each box will have a theme like a Dinosaur box, Kaiju Box, Scifi Box, or a Horror box.
